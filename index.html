My name is Madison Buhse and I am a Junior UX Designer. I am passionate about users and making beautiful designs that are delightful to use. I studied Architectural Design at California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo, for about three years. My studies there solidified my design process and involved making many digital posters and graphics. Those projects and experiences gave me a strong background in visual design and taught me how to integrate a business' needs with the users’ needs throughout each project. Aside from my time at Cal Poly, I participated in a UX/UI Bootcamp through University of Texas at Austin, where I received a certificate of UX/UI design. Through that bootcamp I gained experience in UX research, information architecture, prototyping and wireframing, visual design, and basic app development. I completed a handful of projects in the bootcamp, ranging from mobile apps to e-commerce websites. These experiences have strengthened my passion for design and inspired me to make designs that are intuitive and enjoyable for every user.
